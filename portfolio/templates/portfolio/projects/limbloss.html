Limbloss

A DAE Game Project (2023)

Limbloss is a game project developed by me and 5 other students as part of our Digital Arts and Entertainment education at Howest Kortrijk.
The game is a 3D brawler type game where you play as a robot knight with as goal to cut off the limbs of your opponent. The last robot standing wins

I worked on the physics based movement of the characters, where the animations are wonky based on the speed of the character.

The game dev and download links can be found on Itch.io

The game was also entered as an entry to The rookies
